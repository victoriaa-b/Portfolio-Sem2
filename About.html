About Me

Hi, I'm Victoria, an aspiring software developer from Conception Bay South, Newfoundland. Currently, I'm pursuing a Software Development program at Keyin College, having previously completed a bachelor's degree at university. This isn't my first post-secondary education; after graduation, I felt the need to dive deeper into my interests to find something I truly loved.

My journey into technology wasn't typical. Initially, I wanted to work with my best friend for the summer, so I applied for a job. I never expected that this opportunity would completely change my life. It opened my eyes to a new world, and suddenly, computer programming became one of my favorite subjects.

In my personal life, I'm pretty laid back. I work part-time as a programming instructor while continuing my studies. When I'm not at work or in class, you'll find me in a cozy spot, reading with my pug, Deino, or planning my next trip. I love traveling to different parts of Canada and Europe, though I travel less often now because Deino isn't a fan of leaving the house.
